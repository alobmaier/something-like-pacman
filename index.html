Something like Pacman!

CONTROLS

Use your arrow keys to navigate Pacman.
Press P to pause the game and click or press P again to resume.

GHOSTS

BLINKY

Blinky is the most aggressive ghost. He chases Pacman right away and aims directly to his position.

PINKY

Pinky is cool. And pink! And yes, he chases Pacman right from the start, but doesn't aim directly at him ;-)

INKY

Inky starts chasing Pacman, when he has already eaten 30 Pills.

CLYDE

Clyde starts chasing Pacman when he has eaten more than 2/3 of all the pills.
